Types of Giutars

There are several main types of guitars, each with its own sound and purpose:

Acoustic Guitar

Known for its warm, natural tone. Perfect for beginners and singer-songwriters.

Electric Guitar

Used in rock, metal, blues and jazz. Requires an amplifier.

Classical Guitar

Uses nylon strings and is ideal for classical and flamenco music.

Bass Guitar

Provides the low-end foundation in bands. Comes in 4-string and 5-string versions.
