Tips for Beginners

  • Practice regularly, even 10-15 minutes a day.
  • Start slowly and increase speed over time.
  • Learn basic chords before advanced techniques.
  • Use a metronome to improve rhythm.
  • Keep your guitar in tune.
  • Don't press too hard - relax your hand.
