Tips for Beginners

1. Practice Every Day

Even 10-15 minutes daily is better than 2 hours once a week.

2. Start Slow

Speed comes naturally. Focus on clean sound and correct finger placement.

3. Learn Basic Chords First

C, G, D, E, A - these chords unlock hundreds of songs.

4. Use a Metronome

It helps you develop rhythm and timing.

5. Keep Your Guitar in Tune

A well-tuned guitar motivates you to play more.

6. Relax Your Hands

Dont't press too hard. Tension slows you down.

7. Learn Songs You Enjoy

Motivation is the key to progress.
