Types of Giutars

There are several main types of guitars, each with its own sound and purpose:

Acoustic Guitar

The acoustic guitar produces sound acoustically by transmitting the vibration of the strings to the air.It's known for its warm, natural tone. Perfect for beginners and singer-songwriters.

Electric Guitar

Electric guitars use pickups to convert string vibrations into electrical signals. It's used in rock, metal, blues and jazz. Requires an amplifier.

Classical Guitar

Classical guitars use nylon strings and have a warm, mellow tone.They are ideal for classical and flamenco music.

Bass Guitar

The bass guitar provides the low-end foundation in most modern music genres. Comes in 4-string and 5-string versions.
