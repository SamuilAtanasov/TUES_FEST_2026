Basic chords

Here are some of the most common beginner chords:

  • C Major - one of the easiest chords
  • C Seven - used in blues, jazz, and songs that need a strong resolution
  • G Major - very common in songs
  • G Seven - warm, classic sound and is common in blues, jazz, and pop progressions
  • D Major - bright and clean sound
  • D Minor - sad, dramatic tone and is common in classical and ballad music
  • D Seven - bright, slightly bluesy sound and is used in folk, country, and jazz
  • E Major - simple and emotional
  • E Minor - soft, emotional sound and appears in many modern songs
  • E Seven - used in blues, rock, and jazz to create tension before resolving
  • A Major - perfect for beginners
  • A Minor - soft, melancholic sound and appears in countless songs across all genres
  • A Seven - very common in blues, rock, and jazz progressions
